# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove or edit content, or restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
